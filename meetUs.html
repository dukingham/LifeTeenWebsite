Get to Know Us!

As Stated on our Home page, Life Teen is devoted to Leading teens closer to Christ. The way we do that is through relational ministry. We get to know each and everyone of our teens and their individual needs. we meet them where they're at and invite them into a deeper relationship with Christ.
