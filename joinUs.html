Join us every Sunday Evening for the 5PM Mass!

Here's what the average night looks like:

WE START WITH THE MASS:

We start every night with Mass! The reason we do this is to instill in our teens the love of the Mass. As catholics we belive that the Mass is the source and the summit of our faith! That is to say that it is where we find the reason for our belief (Jesus in the Eucharist) and it is the highest expression and prayer. The Mass is where we become one with our Lord Jesus Christ.

DINNER AT 6:00:

After Mass we talk for a bit in the back of the church called the Narthex to gather teens as they exit the church after Mass. We then head into the Bulam center for dinner for about a half hour to 40 minutes. Community is a big part of our ministry! We believe that if we have people to grow with in love of Christ our faith will grow stronger.
